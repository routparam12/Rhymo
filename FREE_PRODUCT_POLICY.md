# Rhymo free product policy

Rhymo is free for every listener.

## Never ship

- Premium, Pro, Plus, VIP, or subscription tiers
- Payment or upgrade screens
- Paid-only songs, playlists, lyrics, downloads, skips, or audio quality
- Artificial playback limits intended to force an upgrade
- Premium badges, locked controls, or upgrade advertising

## Product rule

New features must be evaluated as one shared experience for all users. UI copy, navigation, analytics events, remote configuration, and backend schemas must not introduce entitlement or subscription state.

Music licensing and infrastructure costs remain launch requirements, but they must not silently turn free product features into paid features.
